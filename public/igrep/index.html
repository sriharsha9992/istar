New job

Assembled genomes of 17 species have been collected from ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes and pre-loaded into memory. Their genome sizes vary from 0.19Gbp to 3.50Gbp. An igrep query consists of a pattern of alphabet A, C, G, T, N and an edit distance. N is a wildcard and must match either A, C, G, or T in the genome. Substitution, insertion and deletion have a uniform cost of one edit distance. The pattern length must be between 1 and 64, and the edit distance must be between 0 and 9, and must not exceed the pattern length. For each query, a maximum of 10,000 ending positions of matches will be returned. For each job, up to 100 queries will be processed.

Citation
